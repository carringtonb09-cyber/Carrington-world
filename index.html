About

The man behind
the kit

Carrington Brown is a world-class musical director and producer whose artistry has graced the biggest stages in music. From a church stage at age two to a full scholarship at Berklee College of Music, his path has been defined by relentless pursuit of excellence.

In 2019, he took the GRAMMY Awards stage alongside H.E.R., cementing his place among the elite. Since then, he has served as Musical Director for Teyana Taylor, led arenas worldwide with Lil Baby on the WHAM World Tour, held it down for NBA YoungBoy, and collaborated with artists spanning Latto and GloRilla.

His sound has been shaped by the intersection of gospel, R&B, hip-hop, and soul. A dual AFM/SAG-AFTRA member operating at the highest levels of the music industry, Carrington brings the vision, the energy, and the precision that turns a good show into an unforgettable one.
